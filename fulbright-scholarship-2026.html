Fulbright Scholarship 2026

The Fulbright Program is a prestigious U.S. government scholarship that enables international students to pursue graduate studies, research, or teaching assistantships in the United States.
It promotes cultural exchange and leadership development between the United States and other countries.

What it covers

✔ Tuition fees
✔ Living stipend
✔ Airfare
✔ Health insurance

Eligibility

- Strong academic record
- Leadership potential
- Commitment to return to home country after studies
