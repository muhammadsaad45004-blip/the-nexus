Erasmus Mundus Scholarship

Erasmus Mundus is a European Union funded scholarship program that allows students to study in multiple universities across different European countries under one joint master’s degree.
It is one of the most unique scholarship programs in the world because students move between 2–3 countries during their degree.

What it includes

✔ Full tuition coverage
✔ Monthly stipend
✔ Travel allowance
✔ Insurance coverage

Why students love it

- Study in multiple countries
- International exposure
- High employability after graduation
