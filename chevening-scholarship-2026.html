Chevening Scholarship 2026

The Chevening Scholarship is a fully funded UK government program that allows outstanding students from around the world to pursue a one-year master’s degree in the United Kingdom.
It is designed for individuals with leadership potential, strong academic background, and a vision to bring positive change in their home country after completing their studies.

What it covers

✔ Full tuition fees
✔ Monthly living allowance
✔ Travel costs to/from UK
✔ Visa fees and support

Basic Requirements

- Undergraduate degree
- Minimum 2 years work experience
- Strong leadership qualities
- English language proficiency
Chevening is highly competitive, so strong essays and leadership examples are essential for success.
